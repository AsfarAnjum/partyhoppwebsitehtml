FAQ

Most frequent questions and answers

Is PartyHop only meant for parties?

No! PartyHop is an experiment for students to redefine what a “party” really means. Anything people decide to be a party they feel would benefit from the app can be promoted. Examples include but are not limited to: Pick-up basketball games, Smash Bro tourneys, kickbacks, board game nights, LAN parties, themed parties/special events, events created by clubs on or off-campus, or fundraisers.

What happens if an address is posted incorrectly or I find an event that is empty?

You can either write a message in the live chat of the event to tell others that the event has been cancelled or is incorrect or make sure to email partyhopbiz@gmail.com   so the host can be dealt with accordingly

Will anyone be able to view my event?

During our beta stages yes, we are currency only asking people to promote their parties near or around the University areas to make the app a more controlled environment during our beta stages

Will What happens if I made a duplicate party or made something by mistake?

Go to Host Profile on the profile tab>then tap on the event that needs to be deleted> scroll down and then tap cancel event.
